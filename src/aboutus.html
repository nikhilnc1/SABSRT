About Us

We SABSRT Industries are a leading provider of engineered foams, tapes, masking solutions, gaskets, surface protection films, and acoustic products dedicated to creating sustainable value for our customers. Founded in 2022, SABSRT Industries has rapidly established itself as a premier provider of these engineered materials. Our products are meticulously designed to meet customer requirements, offering superior general physical properties (GPP) analysis. Our mission is to bring high-quality, sustainable materials to industries worldwide, fostering value through innovative and reliable solutions tailored to our clients' needs. Our vision is simple yet ambitious: to be at the forefront of the industrial materials sector, providing solutions that are both versatile and sustainable. We believe in developing products that not only meet the immediate demands of our clients but also anticipate the future, promoting a sustainable environment and business landscape. Starting from humble beginnings, SABSRT Industries has grown by focusing on our core principles: quality, innovation, and customer commitment. Our team of engineers and experts work in state-of-the-art facilities, equipped with cutting-edge technology and modern machinery, to ensure our products stand out in the market. Every material, from engineered foams to surface protection films, undergoes stringent testing and quality control processes. This meticulous approach guarantees that each product performs optimally, regardless of the industry or application.

Our Vision

Incorporated in the year 2022, SABSRT Industries was started with a vision to be a leading provider of innovative, high-quality engineered materials that cater to the evolving needs of industries worldwide. We are committed to achieving excellence through continuous improvement, technological advancements, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. By consistently meeting and exceeding customer expectations, we aim to contribute to the success and growth of our clients, ensuring mutual prosperity and a sustainable future for all.

Our Facilities

SABSRT Industries boasts a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with modern, high-tech machinery and technologies. Our primary goal is to provide products that are not only reliable and durable but also cost-effective, meeting the diverse needs of our clients. In response to the growing demand for lightweight cellular engineering materials (LCEM), we offer a wide range of dimensions, specifications, thicknesses, and other parameters tailored to customer requirements.
