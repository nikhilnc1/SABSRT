About Us

We SABSRT Industries are a leading provider of engineered foams, tapes, masking solutions, gaskets, surface protection films, and acoustic products dedicated to creating sustainable value for our customers. Our products are meticulously designed to meet customer requirements, offering superior general physical properties (GPP) analysis.

Our Vision

Incorporated in the year 2022, SABSRT Industries was started with a vision to be a leading provider of innovative, high-quality engineered materials that cater to the evolving needs of industries worldwide. We are committed to achieving excellence through continuous improvement, technological advancements, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. By consistently meeting and exceeding customer expectations, we aim to contribute to the success and growth of our clients, ensuring mutual prosperity and a sustainable future for all. "Driving Success Through Innovation and Quality."

Our Facilities

SABSRT Industries boasts a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with modern, high-tech machinery and technologies. Our primary goal is to provide products that are not only reliable and durable but also cost-effective, meeting the diverse needs of our clients. In response to the growing demand for lightweight cellular engineering materials (LCEM), we offer a wide range of dimensions, specifications, thicknesses, and other parameters tailored to customer requirements. "Tailored Solutions for a Brighter Tomorrow."
